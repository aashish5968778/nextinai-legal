Delete Your NextinAI Account

If you would like to delete your NextinAI account and associated data, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to support@nextinai.com with the subject line "Delete My Account".
  2. Include the email address linked to your NextinAI account.
  3. Our team will confirm your request and delete your account and associated data within 7 days.

Data deleted: Email address, account information, saved preferences.

Data retained: None, except where legally required (e.g., fraud prevention, legal compliance).
